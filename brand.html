Voice & Tone

Ferrosa's voice is confident and direct. We talk about real engineering tradeoffs, not marketing fluff. We respect the user's intelligence.

⚙

Technical

Lead with specifics. "Lock-free reads via ArcSwap" not "blazing fast performance."

⚡

Direct

Short sentences. Active voice. State the benefit, then the mechanism.

⚖

Honest

Acknowledge tradeoffs. S3 adds read latency on cache miss — we say so.

🛠

Builder-First

Written for engineers who will evaluate the code, not executives reading a slide deck.

Logo Usage

Do

Use the full logo on dark backgrounds (#0a0a0f to #16161f). Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "Fe" mark on all sides. Use the icon mark alone when space is tight (favicons, avatars, badges).

Don't

Don't place the logo on busy backgrounds or light surfaces without inverting colors. Don't stretch, rotate, or recolor the gradient. Don't add effects like drop shadows or outer glows.

Minimum Size

Full logo: 120px wide minimum. Icon mark: 24px minimum. Favicon: 16px (use favicon.svg which is optimized for small sizes).

Co-Branding

When shown alongside partner logos, maintain equal visual weight. Separate with a vertical divider (1px, var(--border) color) with 24px horizontal padding.

Naming

Correct

Ferrosa (capitalized, not all-caps). Lowercase "ferrosa" in code contexts (package names, CLI). "The Ferrosa Project" for the community. Crate names use ferrosa- prefix.

Incorrect

FERROSA, FerrosaDB, Ferrosa DB (two words), ferrosa.io (not a domain we control). Never abbreviate to "Ferro" or "FSA."
